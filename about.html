KNOT

KNOT is a collective of 4 artists working with real-time composition and textuality.

Ettore Biagi
clarinets, mezmar, spoken poetry
Tian Fu
electronics, rap, texts
Aya Masui
electronics, snare drum, objects
Vlad Ceban
storyteller

The group is composed of a writer, a clarinetist/visual artist/poet, a percussionist/sound artist and a composer/rapper. Our practice is based on instrumental electro-acoustic improvisation, spoken poetry, freestyle Chinese rap, field recordings and sampling. KNOT's performances encompass interrupted reportage and a form of multilingual speculative storytelling, exploring the counterpoint of factual and virtual.
